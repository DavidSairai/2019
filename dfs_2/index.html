What I Do

I support and manage Microsoft SCCM 2007 and SMS 2003 for +3 000 South African stores and +200 out of South Africa stores I also manage and resolve risks using Symantec Endpoint Protection in the all stores environment. My other duties are also the Stores Patch Coordinator where I co-ordinate the patching and updating of the stores environment using WSUS and SCCM
